Tokenisation of Bonds on the Blockchain

We provide the first tokenisation service, for financial assets. Using the power of the Blockchain to empower digitalized asset's ownership. As innovators, our firm takes advantage of the power of smart-contracts and non-fungible tokens, to create the new generation of financial tools.

How does it work?

Connect your wallet, and proceed to buy the digital asset containing all the bond informations. The asset will be directly sent to your wallet. The Bond is loaded on the blockchain though the use of a ERC-721 smart contract, providing high standards of security, traceability and secure for transactions, guaranteeing the safety of an "escrow" like service, but instant, democratic and 100% controlled by the Blockchain. Owning the BOND-NFT, provides the same benefits of owning it's physical equivalent.

ERC-720 Smart Contracts

ERC is the acronym for, Ethereum Request for Comments (ERC) 721 is a data standard for creating non fungible tokens, meaning each token is unique and cannot be divided or directly exchanged for another ERC-721 token. this specific contract allows to create a unique digital asset on the blockchain, carrying the bond and the unique contract-id, denying any possibility of counterfeiting.
